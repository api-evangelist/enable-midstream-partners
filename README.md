# Enable Midstream Partners (enable-midstream-partners)

Enable Midstream Partners was a midstream energy company engaged in the gathering, processing, transportation, and storage of natural gas, natural gas liquids, and crude oil. Enable Midstream Partners was acquired by Energy Transfer LP in December 2021 and operations have been integrated into Energy Transfer's midstream business. No public developer APIs are published for this entity.
